# NUS SDS Mini Datathon 2025
This competition is hosted by NUS Statistics and Data Science Society. In this edition, our goal is to predict medical insurance charges based on lifestyle and demographic data and providing insights on the different feature variables. Our team decided to perform our analysis in R.

  6. Fairness Analysis
